      1 .TH SOWM 1 sowm\-VERSION
      2 .SH NAME
      3 sowm \- Simple Opinionated Window Manager
      4 .SH SYNOPSIS
      5 .B dwm
      6 .RB [ \-v ]
      7 .SH DESCRIPTION
      8 sowm is an itsy bitsy floating window manager (220~ sloc / 24kb compiled!).
      9 .SH USAGE
     10 .SS Keyboard commands
     11 .TP
     12 .B Mod4\-Return
     13 Start
     14 .BR st(1).
     15 .TP
     16 .B Mod4\-d
     17 Spawn
     18 .BR dmenu(1)
     19 for launching other programs.
     20 .TP
     21 .B Mod4\m,
     22 lauchs ncmpcpp.
     23 .TP
     24 .B Mod4\p.
     25 lauches pulsemixer.
     26 .TP
     27 .B Mod4\-h
     28 move focused window to the left.
     29 .TP
     30 .B Mod4\-j
     31 move focused window to down.
     32 .TP
     33 .B Mod4\-k
     34 move focused window to up.
     35 .TP
     36 .B Mod4\-l
     37 move focused window to the right.
     38 .TP
     39 .B Mod4\-Shift\-h
     40 shrink the window from the right.
     41 .TP
     42 .B Mod4\-Shift\-j
     43 shrink the window from the top.
     44 .TP
     45 .B Mod4\-Shift\-k
     46 grow the window from the top.
     47 .TP
     48 .B Mod4\-Shift\-l
     49 grow the window from the right.
     50 .TP
     51 .B Mod4\-Tab
     52 Send focused window to next screen, if any.
     53 .TP
     54 .B Mod4\-Shift\-Tab
     55 Send focused window to previous screen, if any.
     56 .TP
     57 .B Mod4\-Shift\-q
     58 Kill focused window
     59 .SS Mouse commands
     60 .TP
     61 .B Mod4\-Button1
     62 Drag window.
     63 .TP
     64 .B Mod1\-Button2
     65 Resize window.
     66 .SH CUSTOMIZATION
     67 sowm is customized by creating a custom config.h and (re)compiling the source
     68 code.
     69 .SH SEE ALSO
